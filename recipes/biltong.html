Biltong

Dried meat snack

Biltong is a form of dried, cured meat which originated in Southern African countries. Various types of meat are used to produce it

Ingredients

  • Beef cut
  • Salt and vinegar
  • Spices and coriander

Steps

  1. Dry meat with paper towel
  2. Add together vinegar, salt and spices
  3. Hang to dry in biltong cabinet
